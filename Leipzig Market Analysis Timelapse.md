## Executive Summary

Baustellen-Timelapse Leipzig enters a fragmented but viable German market. Competition is split between security-focused providers, webcam/documentation platforms, and film/timelapse specialists. The key opportunity lies in combining **documentation + storytelling + local service + premium output**.

Key findings:
- Market pricing anchors: ~€180–€300/month standard, up to €600+ for advanced systems
- Leipzig + 100km region offers strong, underserved niche positioning
- Differentiation should focus on **visual quality + communication value**, not hardware
